Search Inside Yourself Chapter 3 Reflection

What I learnt from the Mindfulness Conversation:

I was nervous at first, but during it I found it really nice to be able to listen to my friend. I realised that I hadn't properly spoken to someone in this sort of way in such a long time. It was a really great bonding experience, and I would do it again. The other person found it very good, and says they feel like they got a deeper understanding and connection with me, and that it strengthened our friendship.

Techniques I will use to sustain my practice:

Two techniques I will be continuing daily are do less than you can and one breath a day. These stood out to me as helpful for me because they aren't too overwhelming, and don't feel like a big commitment that I will soon lose track of. I like the idea that I can generate an intention to do something kind and beneficial for myself each day, and over time that self directed kindness can become easier, which will help make mindfulness easier too.

Other Musings:

I was really pleasantly surprised by the mindfulness conversation, and I infact tried it out again later in the week, during a very tense situation where I had to have a seriou discussion with my parents, and it went so much better than I thought it would. I am pleased that this book is already starting to have a positive impact on my life.

Search Inside Yourself Chapter 5 Reflection

Triggers when programming:

When I am programming, here are some things that can evoke a disproportionate emotional response:

  • Distracting noises: these can make me very irritated rather quickly
  • Big blocks of unfamiliar syntax: this can sometimes overwhelm me, I feel like I'm looking at heiroglyphics.
  • Time Restrictions: When I know I will have to spend less time than I'd like to on a particular exercise, it makes me feel stressed, and I end up having even less time to things on account of the stress.

SiBerian North RailRoad exercise:

I found this exercise helpful. I encountered loud noise due to the wind fiercely blowing outside, knocking things around. This distracted me from my coding, and made me feel very annoyed. I felt it in my ears and in my chest. I used the exercise to help me calm down. I found the part where you gather all of the emotion in your fist and let it go very helpful.

Thoughts On Shereef's Fireside Chat

The EDA Experience So Far:

I've had a highly positive experience so far. I really enjoy the teamwork and emotional intelligence aspect, and I am excited about the prospect of becoming excellent at web development and looking for a job.

What I took away from Shereef's video:

What I took away from Shereef's video was the importance of the Kitchen Mindset, the feeling of being in charge of creating the work, and being able to actively participate throughthe ups and downs, the fun parts and the disasters.

How I see myself engaging in EDA culture:

I might be shy at first, but I think it is a supportive space for new learning and growth. At every corner I expect I'll face a lot of challenges, but I hope to meet them head on with integrity, kindness and lots of effort. A big motto of mine is to never give up.

My expectations of EDA:

They have definitely changed. I thought it would be a lot more serious, and I was cynical about the teamwork side, but now that I've started I can see it really works.

How I feel about the learning environment:

I am excited, and nervous to learn in this environment, but it's all helping me to stay productive, so I don't worry about being nervous. I often find myself completely losing track of time, and getting very absorbed in the work. It's only phase 0, so I don't know yet how I'll react to the bootcamp campus learning environment, but I have positive hopes about it.
